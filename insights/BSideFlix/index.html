Cristian Silva

ABSTRACT

Currently, all tools that offer playback of movies and series on demand on the Internet are designed to display large corporations productions, which in many cases inhibits the creation of content from smaller studios, thus hindering the entry of new talent in market.

The Bsideflix is a project to create a social network for independent movies and series fans, with a space for crowdfunding and help producers to makes possible the development of content.

The social network will be aimed at students of areas related to cinema and enthusiasts of series and independent movies.

PROPOSAL

::Movies and series on Demand::

The Bsideflix offer playing content through a mobile APP or by site, and the user will have access to productions by three ways:

●Contributing to the production: The user automatically gets the ticket to watch the series or movie for a period.

●By paying a monthly fee (Can't be expensive): The user has access to all content acquis.

●Paying the film or series individually: The user receives a ticket to watch the movie or series for a period.

Each production will have a team. Teams must register and provide some material to attract the attention of employees.

Examples of materials which may be submitted:

●Trailer

●Teaser

●Script

●Explanatory Video

Each team will make a list of needs for the series or film and employees who wish to see the production completed will select these needs to support.

The team should specify the time for the production to be launched starting from the date on which all the objectives have been achieved.

If production has been canceled, all collected needs must be returned (including money). Examples of production team needs:

●Loan of equipment

●Location

●Accommodation

●Marketing Campaigns Creation

●Web page development

●Actors and actresses

●Cameraman

●Costume designer

●Loan clothes

●Loan objects

Contributions provide the right of access to work, and can reward contributors with participation in the movie, thanks in the credits, VIP invitation to the launch event, etc, according production team choice.

::Social Network::

In addition to offering the movie service and online indie series, the Bsideflix should also have the role of social network where users can interact with the production teams to make suggestions for improvements, and to evaluate the work and contribute to the dissemination of these in their own social networks.
